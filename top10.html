Top 10 Superfoods

These nutritional powerhouses can boost your health dramatically

1. Blueberries

Rich in antioxidants, vitamins C and K, and fiber. May improve brain health and reduce DNA damage.

2. Spinach

Packed with iron, calcium, folate, and fiber. Supports vision, bone health, and immune function.

3. Salmon

Excellent source of omega-3 fatty acids and high-quality protein. Supports heart and brain health.

4. Avocados

Loaded with healthy fats, potassium, and fiber. Promotes heart health and helps absorb nutrients.

5. Greek Yogurt

High in protein and probiotics. Supports gut health, digestion, and muscle maintenance.

6. Broccoli

Contains vitamin C, K, and sulforaphane. Fights inflammation and supports detoxification.

7. Quinoa

A complete plant-based protein rich in fiber, iron, and magnesium. Great for sustained energy.

8. Chia Seeds

High in omega-3s, fiber, and protein. Supports heart health and helps regulate digestion.

9. Turmeric

Contains curcumin, a powerful anti-inflammatory compound. May reduce risk of chronic diseases.

10. Green Tea

Loaded with antioxidants like EGCG. Enhances metabolism, brain function, and fat burning.

Eat smart, live strong!
