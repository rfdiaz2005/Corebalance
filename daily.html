Mindfulness Tips

  • Start with deep breathing: Spend 2 minutes focusing only on your breath. Inhale slowly, hold, then exhale.
  • Daily journaling: Write down 3 things you’re grateful for each day.
  • Mindful walking: Take a 5-10 minute walk while observing nature and your surroundings.
  • Body scan meditation: Close your eyes and mentally check in with each part of your body from head to toe.
  • Digital detox: Take a break from screens for at least 30 minutes to reset your mind.
  • Stretch with intention: Do 5 minutes of yoga or stretching while focusing on how your body feels.
  • Evening reflection: Before bed, reflect on one peaceful moment from your day.
