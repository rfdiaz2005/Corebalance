5-Minute Morning Stretch

Start your day right with these energizing movements

1. Neck Rolls – 30 seconds

Gently roll your neck in slow circles, both clockwise and counterclockwise. Keep your shoulders relaxed.

Benefits: Relieves neck stiffness, improves range of motion, and reduces tension from sleep posture.

2. Shoulder Rolls – 30 seconds

Lift your shoulders up toward your ears, then roll them back and down in a circular motion. Repeat slowly.

Benefits: Warms up shoulder joints, improves posture, and promotes circulation in the upper body.

3. Side Stretch – 30 seconds each side

Stand with feet shoulder-width apart. Raise one arm overhead and lean to the opposite side. Feel the stretch through your side body.

Benefits: Increases flexibility in the spine and torso, improves posture, and stimulates the core muscles.

4. Forward Fold – 1 minute

Stand tall, then bend forward at the hips and let your head hang heavy. Keep a slight bend in your knees if needed.

Benefits: Stretches hamstrings, calves, and lower back. Calms the nervous system and relieves stress.

5. Cat-Cow Stretch – 1 minute

On all fours, alternate between arching your back (cat) and dipping it toward the floor (cow), syncing movement with breath.

Benefits: Improves spinal flexibility, reduces back stiffness, and gently activates the core muscles.

6. Seated Torso Twist – 30 seconds each side

Sit cross-legged or on a chair. Twist your torso to one side, placing one hand behind you and the other on the opposite knee for support.

Benefits: Enhances spinal mobility, stimulates digestion, and promotes better circulation in your organs.

7. Deep Breathing – 30 seconds

Find a comfortable seated or standing position. Inhale deeply through your nose, hold for a second, then exhale slowly through your mouth.

Benefits: Centers your mind, lowers stress, increases oxygen intake, and prepares you mentally for the day.
