Beginner Workouts

Build strength and confidence with simple daily routines designed for everyone.

Workout Tips

Start with Stretching

Begin each session with 5–10 minutes of dynamic stretching to prepare your body.

Bodyweight Basics

Push-ups, squats, and planks help build strength without any equipment.

Stay Hydrated

Drink water before, during, and after workouts to keep energy levels up.

Rest & Recovery

Allow muscles to recover by including rest days in your routine.

Proper Nutrition

Eat balanced meals with protein and healthy carbs to fuel your progress.

Stay Consistent

Commit to regular workouts, aiming for at least 3 sessions per week.

Sample Daily Beginner Routine

  • 5 minutes warm-up (jumping jacks or brisk walking)
  • 10 push-ups (modify with knees down if needed)
  • 15 bodyweight squats
  • 20-second plank
  • 15 lunges (each leg)
  • 5 minutes cool-down stretching

How long should a beginner workout last?
Typically, 20-30 minutes per session is enough to start with.
How often should I work out as a beginner?
Aim for 3-4 times per week with rest days in between.
Do I need any equipment?
No equipment is needed to start — bodyweight exercises work great!
