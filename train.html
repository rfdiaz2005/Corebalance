Strength Training Basics for Beginners

Learn the essentials to build muscle, improve endurance, and avoid injuries in your gym sessions.

What is Strength Training?

Strength training is a form of exercise focused on building muscle through resistance. It includes exercises like lifting weights, using resistance bands, or even bodyweight workouts like push-ups and squats.

Benefits of Strength Training

  • Increases muscle mass and strength
  • Boosts metabolism and fat loss
  • Improves posture, balance, and joint health
  • Helps prevent injuries and reduces risk of chronic disease
  • Enhances mental health and confidence

Beginner Tips

  • Start with light weights and focus on proper form
  • Train major muscle groups (legs, back, chest, arms)
  • Allow at least one day of rest between sessions
  • Warm up before and stretch after your workout
  • Stay consistent and gradually increase intensity

Sample Beginner Routine

  • Bodyweight Squats – 3 sets of 12 reps
  • Push-ups (or knee push-ups) – 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Dumbbell Rows – 3 sets of 12 reps per side
  • Plank Hold – 3 rounds of 30 seconds
  • Cool down with stretching
