FAQs

How long should a beginner workout last?
Typically, 20-30 minutes per session is enough to start with.
How often should I work out as a beginner?
Aim for 3-4 times per week with rest days in between.
Do I need any equipment?
No equipment is needed to start — bodyweight exercises work great!
