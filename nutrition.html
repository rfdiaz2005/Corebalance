Gym Nutrition Tips

Prioritize Protein

Protein helps build and repair muscle. Include chicken, fish, eggs, or plant-based protein in your diet.

Fuel with Carbs

Whole grains, fruits, and veggies provide energy for workouts. Don’t fear healthy carbs!

Stay Hydrated

Water helps regulate body temperature and muscle function. Drink water before, during, and after workouts.

Post-Workout Fuel

Eat protein and carbs after your workout to help recover and build strength.

Why Gym Nutrition Matters

Nutrition is essential for fitness progress. Your body needs fuel to perform, recover, and grow stronger.

Protein supports muscle repair, carbs give energy, and healthy fats balance hormones. Staying hydrated keeps everything functioning at its best.

With the right diet, you’ll recover faster, lift more, and train harder!
